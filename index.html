ROUTE APP - FRONT END DEVELOPER

ROUTE APP
FRONT END DEVELOPER

React.js | Redux | Sass | Chart.js | Axios HTTP | AWS Cognito |

React.js | Redux | Sass
Chart.js | Axios HTTP | AWS Cognito |

Route's platform is a dashboard where merchants go to see revenue and transaction data from customers who bought Route to insure their order. I wireframed and designed the new platform using Adobe Xd and Adobe Illustrator. I built the whole front end of their platform in React.js. I used SASS for the styling on each component, Redux for storing data throughout the whole platform, and Axios HTTP request for connecting to the endpoints to GET, POST, PUT, and DELETE data in the database. Route uses AWS services for hosting. I helped with uploading content to S3, and creating the flow to host the content from S3 through CloudFront, Route 53, CodeBuild, and CodePipeline. I connected the platform to Cognito for authenticating users to signup and login and out of the platform. We use Asana for keeping track of sprints and tasks that need done.
